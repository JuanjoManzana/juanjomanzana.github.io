Privacy Policy

Overview

The App is designed to help you discover and play publicly available TV streams. We do not require you to create an account and we do not directly collect personal information like your name or email inside the App.

Information We May Process

  • Usage & diagnostics: crash reports, performance metrics, device model, OS version, and generic settings used solely to improve stability.
  • Advertising identifiers: if ads are shown, third-party ad partners may access your device’s advertising ID to provide and measure ads. You can reset/limit this in your device settings.
  • Network requests: to load channel lists, logos, and streams your device contacts our or third-party servers. Server logs may include IP address and timestamps, retained for a limited period for security and operations.

Third-Party Services

The App may integrate third-party SDKs or services (e.g., advertising, analytics, crash reporting, stream hosting). These parties act as independent controllers or processors of data under their own policies. Examples include (not exhaustive):

  • Advertising networks (e.g., Google AdMob)
  • Crash reporting/analytics (e.g., Firebase Crashlytics)
  • Content/CDN providers that host logos or streams

Please refer to the respective provider’s privacy documentation for details on what they collect and why.

Cookies & Similar Technologies

The App itself does not set browser cookies. Third-party content or web pages opened from the App may use cookies or similar technologies.

Children’s Privacy

The App is not directed to children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information to us, contact us and we will delete it.

Data Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect data. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

International Transfers

Depending on where you are, your data may be processed on servers located in other countries that may have different data protection laws.

Your Rights (UK/EEA)

If you are in the UK/EEA, you may have rights to access, rectify, erase, restrict or object to processing, and data portability, where applicable. You can exercise these by contacting us.

Data Retention

We keep diagnostic logs only as long as needed for troubleshooting and to meet legal or security obligations, after which they are deleted or anonymized.

FAQ

The stream doesn’t play or buffers a lot

Try another channel source, switch from mobile data to Wi-Fi, or check if the source is temporarily offline. Some streams are geo-restricted by their owners.

How do I request data deletion?

Send an email to juliobautista202020@gmail.com with subject Data Deletion Request and include your device model and any relevant IDs. We will delete diagnostic records that are reasonably linkable to your device, if any.

Ads are not relevant

You can reset/limit your device’s advertising identifier in system settings (Android: Google → Ads; iOS: Privacy & Security → Tracking).

Report a channel or copyright concern

Email us the channel name, URL (if visible), and details of the issue. We will review and, where appropriate, remove or replace sources.
